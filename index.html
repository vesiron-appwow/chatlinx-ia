CHATLINX GUIDE
Quick Start Guide
ChatLinx lets you continue any AI conversation without restarting or re-explaining. It turns messy thinking into structured, reusable context and keeps it moving forward.
The Loop
1
Create a task — add a name and dump your current thinking. Messy is fine. Don't structure it.
2
Run Distil ⚡ — extracts current state, decisions, open items and next actions automatically.
3
Copy Context Pack — click Copy Full Pack. This is your structured brief, ready to paste.
4
Paste into any AI — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini. The AI now understands everything instantly. Ask for next steps — do not re-explain.
5
Paste the response back ↩ — click Paste AI, paste the response. It merges automatically into your task. Distil re-runs. Loop continues.
Key Rules
Do not rewrite context manually — always use the Context Pack
Always paste AI responses back — this is what prevents context loss
Press N anywhere to create a new task instantly
What Makes It Work
Tasks hold state — not chat threads
Distil creates clarity from mess
Context Pack transfers intelligence to any AI
The loop maintains continuity indefinitely
ChatLinx does not execute AI calls. It prepares, structures and manages your AI workflow. You interact directly with your chosen AI platform.
